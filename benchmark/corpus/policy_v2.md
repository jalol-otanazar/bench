# Nimbus Internal Policy v2

**Effective:** 2026-02-01

## Security and data handling
- Passwords must be reset every **60 days**.
- Public AI tools may be used only for **public docs**, **non-sensitive internal training drafts**, and **brainstorming public blog titles**.
- Public AI tools may **not** be used with customer data, credentials, or contract redlines.
- Internal support logs are retained for **14 days**.

## Support targets
- **P1** incidents: first response within **20 minutes**.
- **P2** incidents: first response within **2 hours**.

## Expense policy
- Meals above **$50** require manager approval.
