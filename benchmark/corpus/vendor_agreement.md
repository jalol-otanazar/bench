# Nimbus Vendor Agreement: Acme Data

## Data handling
- EU customer data is stored in **Frankfurt**.
- Breach notice must be sent within **48 hours**.
- Subprocessors require **written approval**.

## Support
- Support tickets are responded to within **1 business day**.
- The agreement does not promise any specific phone coverage window.

## Retention
- Vendor logs are retained for **90 days**.
