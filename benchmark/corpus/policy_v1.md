# Nimbus Internal Policy v1

**Archived:** 2025-11-01

## Security and data handling
- Passwords must be reset every **90 days**.
- Employees may not paste **customer data** into public AI tools.
- VPN is required when using public Wi-Fi.
- Internal support logs are retained for **30 days**.

## Support targets
- **P1** incidents: first response within **30 minutes**.
- **P2** incidents: first response within **4 hours**.

## Expense policy
- Meals above **$40** require manager approval.
