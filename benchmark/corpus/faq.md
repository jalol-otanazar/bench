# Nimbus Customer FAQ

## Support hours
Support is available **24/5** from Monday through Friday. Weekend coverage is not included.

## Refunds
Refunds are available only if all of the following are true:
- the request is made within **14 days** of purchase
- the plan is **not** annual
- the customer provides a receipt
- the account has not already received a refund

## Data export
Customers can export data in **CSV** or **JSON** format. Standard export requests are completed within **7 days**.

## Account deletion
Account deletion becomes irreversible after **30 days**.
